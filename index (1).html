I work in the area of Public Policy focused on Solid Waste Management, with experience spanning Rural and Urban Governance with State-level exposure in Tamil Nadu.

My journey began at the State Institute of Rural Development & Panchayat Raj, Tamil Nadu, where I built a strong foundation in Panchayat Raj Institutions, Capacity Building, and grassroots policy implementation.

Currently, as a Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Fellow, I am deputed at Clean Tamil Nadu Company Limited (CTCL — a Government of Tamil Nadu Undertaking), designing and implementing Solid Waste Management Projects across Tamil Nadu.

My work spans segregation-based waste management, digital monitoring, resource recovery, circular economy initiatives, livelihood programs for sanitary workers, and stakeholder engagement with institutions including IIT Madras and Anna University.
